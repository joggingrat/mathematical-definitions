Below is a grammar for the language I used. All "$`\cdots`$" may be omitted.

$`\langle \mathrm{variable}\rangle\Rightarrow x|y|z|A|B|C|\alpha|\beta|\gamma|\cdots`$\
$`\langle \mathrm{function_{0ar}}\rangle\Rightarrow 0|\varnothing|\omega|\cdots`$\
$`\langle \mathrm{function_{1ar}}\rangle\Rightarrow D|R|P|\aleph|\cdots`$\
$`\langle \mathrm{function_{2ar}}\rangle\Rightarrow \cap|\cup|\sim|\upharpoonright|Map|\cdots`$\
$`\langle \mathrm{term}\rangle\Rightarrow`$\
$`\qquad\qquad\ \ \langle\mathrm{funcion_{0ar}}\rangle|
\langle \mathrm{funcion_{1ar}}\rangle(\langle term\rangle)|
\langle \mathrm{funcion_{2ar}}\rangle(\langle term\rangle\langle term\rangle)`$\
$`\langle \mathrm{predic_{0ar}}\rangle\Rightarrow \top|\bot`$\
$`\langle \mathrm{predic_{1ar}}\rangle\Rightarrow isSet|isRea|isFun|isOrd`$
